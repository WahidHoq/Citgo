Wireline Lubricants

CITGO Wireline Lubricants are premium viscous, tacky lubricants and sealants designed specifically for use in in oil and gas well logging operations and other wireline applications where a lubricating dynamic seal is required. Formulated with advanced corrosion inhibitors to protect against corrosion in harsh high pressure down-hole environments where exposure to moisture, H2S, and corrosive fluids occur. CITGO Wireline Lubricants are available in several viscosity grades for ease of application in varying temperature conditions.
